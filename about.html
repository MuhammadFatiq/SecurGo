About Me

Cybersecurity & Digital Forensics Student at XYZ Company

Hello! I am Fatiq, a current Cybersecurity & Digital Forensics student at XYZ company. I have a strong passion for all things related to Cybersecurity as well as technology, with a keen interest in protecting individuals and organisations from cyber threats.

Ever since I was a child, I've been fascinated by the endless potential of technology to shape our lives. I vividly remember using my tablet as a 7-year old to learn how to draw! As I grew older, I became increasingly aware of the growing importance of cybersecurity in our digital age. With such a high reliance on technology in our daily lives, the need for robust cybersecurity practices has never been more critical.

In my free time, you can often find me exploring different developments in programming with Python, tinkering with different tech gadgets and immersing myself in various research topics.

In this blog, I aim to serve up bite-sized, easy-to-understand information on various aspects of cybersecurity. My goal is to provide valuable insights and resources that can empower individuals (and maybe even on a greater scale!) to enhance their cybersecurity posture without getting stranded in the technical jargon.

I will serve three types of posts: Secur Shield with bite-sized, easy-to-understand content accessible for all readers, and with more technical and in-depth insights, and other posts that are more related to documenting my cybersecurity journey.

Thank you for joining me on this cybersecurity journey! I look forward to sharing my knowledge about cybersecurity here.

Stay secure, stay savvy!
